Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General

  • What is the MMTTP Faculty Development Programme?
    It’s a capacity-building initiative that equips educators with AI knowledge and tools.
  • What is MMTTP?
    Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Programme, aligned with NEP 2020 to transform education.
  • Why is AI important for educators?
    AI helps personalize learning, automate tasks, and enhance accessibility and thinking skills.
  • What is the Links Repository?
    A centralized link hub. Open Repository

Program Structure

  • The FDP runs for 4 weeks, 5 days a week, 1 module per day.
  • Each module includes a live session, video content, and activities.
  • Live sessions run from 9 AM – 12 PM.
  • Attendance and activity completion are mandatory for module credit.

Modules

  • A module includes a Zoom session, video, and activities on the website.
  • You must pass attendance, poll participation, and submit all activities.

Zoom Live Sessions

  • Attend using your own registered Zoom link.
  • Stay logged in during breaks; breakout rooms are required.
  • Do not share Zoom links or record sessions.

Activities

  • Submit all listed activities by the deadline: 25 April 2025, 11:59 PM.
  • Submit via the official Google Form only.
  • Instructions are given next to each activity.
  • In special cases, progress and issues can be uploaded for review.
  • Website: MMTTP Activity Portal

MEME (Multiple Entry Multiple Exit Policy)

  • You can attend and complete modules across different weeks.
  • Order of completion does not matter.
  • Certification requires all 5 modules completed successfully.

Certification

  • Free of cost
  • Requires successful completion of all 5 modules
  • Certificates are sent via email after processing
  • Not guaranteed without full participation
